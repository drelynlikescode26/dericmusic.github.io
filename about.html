About

Bio

Deric is a singer, songwriter, and producer crafting cinematic R&B from the quiet side of midnight. His music moves through late-night emotion and real-life stories — built for the moments that are hard to put into words.

His 2026 project, Mood Swings & Melodies III, marked the arrival of a distinct artistic identity: introspective lyricism layered over immersive soundscapes. Each release is a document of a specific emotional state, crafted with the kind of patience that only comes from making music you actually mean.

Deric writes, records, and produces on his own terms — choosing feeling over formula, atmosphere over noise. The music follows no rules except honesty.
