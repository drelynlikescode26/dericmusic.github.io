Short Bio (80 words)

Deric is a singer, songwriter, and producer crafting cinematic R&B from the quiet side of midnight. His music moves through late-night emotion and real-life stories — built for the moments that are hard to put into words. His 2026 debut project, Mood Swings & Melodies III, established his voice as one of measured vulnerability and sonic depth. Based independently, Deric writes and produces everything himself, releasing music on his own terms.
