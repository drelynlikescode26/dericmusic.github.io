Short Bio

Deric (born Drelyn Walker) is an Atlanta-based recording artist and songwriter blending R&B, melodic rap, and emotional storytelling. Known for a vocal tone often compared to Don Toliver, Chris Brown, and PnB Rock, Deric has amassed over 20 million streams independently across Spotify, Apple Music, and more — earning editorial placements including Spotify's Fresh Finds. His catalog spans the Mood Swings & Melodies series alongside a growing collection of singles and collaborations with artists and producers at the forefront of the industry. Operating as a fully independent artist, Deric continues to build his sound, his brand, and his audience on his own terms.
